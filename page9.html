NATIONAL SECRETARIAT: 

The secretariat is the national administrative hub for the organization and is headed by  the National President. The secretariat consist of the office of the National President of WAICF, Secretary, Staffs, Volunteers, Researchers and consultant. 

Mandate:

The secretariat is to serve as the accounting, coordinating and controlling instrument of  the day-to-day operations of the WIACF and to work within the administrative and governance structure to deliver a successful project within the time of its mandate 

 Ensuring meetings are effectively organized and reported.

 To plan and convey meetings

 Maintaining effective records and administration

 Upholding legal requirements

 To ensure effective Communication and correspondence.

 To prepare letters, requests, other documents, etc.

 Receive phone calls, letters, emails and correspondence 

 To Control the timely performance of the assignments and tasks assigned to all 

 To meet with and report to the Advisory Board/Steering Committee as often as 
necessary.
