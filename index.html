WOMEN IN AGRICULTURE COOPERATIVE FEDERATION

Women In Agriculture Cooperative Federation (WIACF) is an organization, initiated to bring farmer together from across the federation to carryout agricultural value chain business and comprehensively address critical agriculture problems in an efficient and timely manner that ensures transprency and accountability and equlity across Nigeria. The Vision and Mission and the Objectives of WIACF initiative are in consonance with the board vision and objective of the Nigeria greatest Agricuture Revolution. 

OUR VISION OUR MISSION OBJECTIVES

For effective promotion and coordination of our members to engage in agricultural
trading, locally and internationally, to maximize potential and make agriculture a serious
business in other to improve the standard of living. 

Through several sustainable agricultural programs and activities arranged for all
members, using bottom-top approach to achieve our targeted goals.

MEMBERSHIP

We have membership strength of 6000 in 12 States of the Federation and still spreading.
