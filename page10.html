ZONAL OFFICE EXECUTIVE

This consist of the Zonal Chairman, Vice Chairman, Secretary, Assistant Secretary, Treasurer, Financial Secretary, PRO, Auditor, Ex-official and Project Monitoring Team. 

Mandate:

To Coordinate operations and activities within the zone and give periodic report to the  committee and the National Secretariat.

Functions/Responsibilities

 To coordinate all deliverables of the projects within the zone.

 Supervise activities and projects implementation in the Zone and ensure they  conform to the goals and objectives of WIACF.

 Meet periodically with the State Chairmen and their executives to get first-hand  information and follow up in critical need areas. 

 Report all activities, perspectives, challenges, impart-assessment and  recommendations to the National Secretariat.
