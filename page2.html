OBJECTIVES:

i. To develop a multimedia crowd funding and online identity management  platform for members of cooperative societies and other farmers. 

ii. To develop payment system and monetize social multimedia for members of  cooperative societies and other farmers. 

iii. To develop an online multimedia educational platform for members of  cooperative societies and other farmers.

iv. To develop Geo mapping, smart farms and smart warehouses for members of  cooperative societies and other farmers. 

v. To provide digital Agric extensions services and develop drones for monitoring  members of cooperative society farms. 

vi. To develop E-market place for members, to procure and supply agricultural  inputs (fertilizers, agro-chemical, improved seeds, feeds etc). 

vii. To undertake the multiplication of seeds, seedlings fingerlings, brood stock, in  collaboration with relevant agency/companies for purpose of retailing to members and public. 

viii. To promote and provide mechanization services (tractor hire services, land  clearing) to members of cooperative societies and other farmers. 

ix. To provide financial linkage services and assist cooperative members to access  funding to enhance and expand their agricultural activities. 

x. To provide agricultural haulage services in other to eliminate the pre and  post-harvest losses and transport problems encountered by members of cooperative and other Nigerian farmers which usually give rise to spoilages and destruction of their farm crops and produce. 

xi. To provide agricultural cooperative entrepreneurship development services in  other to build the capacities of agricultural cooperatives societies to ensure their effectiveness and efficiency and to cope with modern technologies. 

xii. To invest its fund in any viable and secured venture that may be beneficial to  the objectives of the organization. 

xiii. To facilitate agricultural processing in Nigeria and to procure and distribute  processing equipment to members. 

xiv. To undertake the marketing and trade in farm produce both locally and  internationally with a view of creating market for members’ product and produce. 

xv. To collaborate with local and international multimedia cooperative  organization and agencies for the promotion and sustenance of global best practices in multimedia agricultural production, processing, storage and marketing. 

xvi. To develop different agricultural insurance services for members of 
cooperative societies and other farmers
