STRATEGIC APPROACH 

To implement the WIACF funding plan to finance activities and programs.

 To call for sensitization and mobilization of interested volunteers in the 36 states  through the WIACF networks and partners to enrol farmers into this movement.

 Call for individual and institutional partnership nationwide for the purpose of  unifying the fight against food insecurity 

 Call for and mobilize financial, non-financial support and partnership on critical need areas like medical interventions, logistics, infrastructures, emergency response, and other related areas 

 Deployment of food and critical home-needs to the most vulnerable people like the widows, orphans, people living with disability and the less privilege.
