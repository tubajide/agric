WIACF NATIONAL ADMINISTRATIVE MANAGEMENT

NATIONAL STEERING COMMITTEE:

The National Steering Committee is the highest  administrative organ for implementing the far reaching objectives of the WIACF Initiative. The committee consist of Eight (8) members, drawn from states and also on the bases of individual capacity and contribution. National President, Secretary General, Financial Controller, Project Manager, Auditor, PRO and HRM. 

Mandate:
To facilitate support and drive the administrative and management component of the WIACF in line with its vision and objectives
Functions/Responsibilities

- Raised funds to finance the organization using the funding strategies.

- Manage and appropriate WIACF in line with the core objectives and goals of its  organization project

- The committee to carry out these functions and responsibilities in consonance with the  promoters of WIACF vision and mission.
